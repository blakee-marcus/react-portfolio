# Client Kickoff Agenda

Use this agenda for the first project call after deposit and intake.

Recommended length: **45–60 minutes**

Primary goal:

> Turn the selected package and intake answers into a confirmed working plan before production begins.

---

## Before the Call

Blake should review:

- Deposit record/package
- Intake responses
- Current website/social links
- Provided assets
- Desired pages
- Timeline constraints
- Any obvious scope questions
- Remaining payment expectations

Prepare:

- Draft page list
- Primary conversion goal
- Known blockers
- Questions that need a decision
- Suggested first milestone

---

## Kickoff Flow

## 1. Welcome + Frame the Call — 5 minutes

Say something like:

> The goal today is to turn your intake into a clear working plan. We’ll confirm the scope, page priorities, timeline, content needs, and next steps so the project can move into production without extra guesswork.

Confirm:

- Client has paid deposit
- Package selected
- Call length
- What will be decided today

---

## 2. Business + Offer Alignment — 10 minutes

Discuss:

- What the business does in plain language
- Highest-priority service/offer
- Ideal client/customer
- Why people choose them
- What should feel clearer after the website launches

Key questions:

1. If a visitor only remembers one thing, what should it be?
2. What service or offer should the site make easiest to choose?
3. What kind of lead/customer would make this project worthwhile?

---

## 3. Website Goal + Conversion Path — 10 minutes

Confirm:

- Primary CTA
- Secondary CTA, if any
- Contact/booking/payment flow
- What happens after someone reaches out
- Trust/proof needed before action

Key questions:

1. What is the main next step for a visitor?
2. What do they need to know before taking that step?
3. What usually stops people from reaching out?

---

## 4. Page List + Scope — 10–15 minutes

Confirm the page list.

For **Essentials**, confirm:

- One-page sections
- Main CTA
- Proof/contact requirements

For **Growth**, confirm:

- Core page list
- Services/proof/about/FAQ/contact needs
- CMS or repeatable content needs

For **Full Brand Build**, confirm:

- Deeper page structure
- Visual/messaging needs
- Motion/polish expectations
- Reusable sections/content system

Scope questions:

1. Are any requested pages outside the package?
2. Are there integrations or features that change scope?
3. What can wait until after launch?

---

## 5. Content + Assets — 10 minutes

Review what exists and what is missing.

Confirm:

- Logo/brand files
- Photos/headshots
- Service descriptions
- Testimonials/reviews
- Legal/contact details
- Links/integrations
- Domain/DNS access if needed

Use checklist: [`client-content-checklist.md`](./client-content-checklist.md)

Key question:

> What content is required for launch, and what can be improved after launch?

---

## 6. Timeline + Review Checkpoints — 5–10 minutes

Confirm:

- Production start date
- Target launch window
- Feedback windows
- Client availability
- Review checkpoints
- What happens if feedback/assets are late

Suggested language:

> The timeline depends on clear feedback and required assets arriving on time. If something is missing, I’ll flag it early so we can decide whether to pause, adjust scope, or launch with a simpler version.

---

## 7. Payment + Admin — 5 minutes

Confirm:

- Deposit was received and applied
- Remaining balance
- When remaining balance is due
- Invoice/payment method
- Any contract/admin steps

Do not give legal advice. If contract terms are needed, use a proper agreement/template reviewed by a professional.

---

## 8. Recap + Next Actions — 5 minutes

End with one clear recap:

- Confirmed package/scope
- Confirmed page list
- Confirmed primary CTA
- Missing assets/content
- First production milestone
- Next action for client
- Next action for Blake

Suggested closing:

> I’ll turn this into a short working plan after the call. Your main next step is [specific action]. Once that is in, I’ll move into [next production step].

---

## Kickoff Definition of Done

Kickoff is complete when:

- [ ] Package and scope are confirmed.
- [ ] Page list is confirmed.
- [ ] Primary CTA is confirmed.
- [ ] Timeline/review checkpoints are confirmed.
- [ ] Required content/assets are identified.
- [ ] Remaining balance/payment expectations are clear.
- [ ] Client has one clear next action.
- [ ] Blake has one clear next production action.
