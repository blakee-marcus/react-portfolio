# Client Email Templates

Reusable email templates for Blake Marcus Studio onboarding, production, launch, and care.

Keep emails warm, direct, and easy to act on.

Rule of thumb:

> One email should have one main client action whenever possible.

---

## 1. Deposit Confirmed / Intake Next

Subject: `Your deposit is confirmed — next step inside`

Hi [First Name],

Your deposit is confirmed — thank you. Your [Package] project is officially in motion.

The next step is to complete the intake form here:

[Intake Link]

This helps me understand your business, your goals, the pages we need, and the content/assets you already have. Rough answers are completely fine — the goal is to get the right context in one place before kickoff.

After intake, book your kickoff call here:

[Kickoff Link]

If either link gives you trouble, just reply here and I’ll help.

— Blake
