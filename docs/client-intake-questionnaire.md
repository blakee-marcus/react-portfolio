# Client Intake Questionnaire

Use this to build the external intake form for Blake Marcus Studio.

Recommended intro copy:

> Thanks for placing your deposit. This intake helps me understand your business, your offer, and what the website needs to do before we kick off. Rough answers are okay. Clear context matters more than perfect wording.

Estimated time: **20–35 minutes**

---

## 1. Contact + Project Basics

**Required**

1. Your full name
2. Email address
3. Business name
4. Business website, if any
5. Social/profile links, if relevant
6. Which package did you choose?
   - Essentials
   - Growth
   - Full Brand Build
7. What is the best way to contact you during the project?
8. Is anyone else involved in approvals? If yes, who?

**Optional**

9. Phone number
10. Business location / service area
11. Preferred pronouns/name spelling

---

## 2. Business Snapshot

1. What does your business do in plain language?
2. Who do you primarily serve?
3. What services or offers matter most right now?
4. What do clients/customers usually come to you for?
5. What makes your business different from similar options?
6. What do people already trust you for?
7. Are there any offers/services you do **not** want to emphasize?

---

## 3. Website Goals

1. Why are you updating/building this website now?
2. What is the main action you want visitors to take?
   - Book a call
   - Fill out a contact form
   - Buy/pay a deposit
   - Visit a location
   - Join a list
   - Learn enough to reach out
   - Other
3. What would make this project feel successful 30 days after launch?
4. What is not working about your current website or online presence?
5. What questions do visitors need answered before they reach out?
6. What objections or hesitations do prospects usually have?

---

## 4. Audience + Buyer Context

1. Describe your ideal client/customer.
2. What situation are they usually in when they look for you?
3. What matters most to them when choosing a provider?
4. What do they need to feel before they contact you?
5. Are there any clients/customers you want less of?
6. What words do your best clients use to describe the problem or desire?

---

## 5. Pages + Content

1. Which pages do you think the website needs?
   - Home
   - Services
   - About/Studio
   - Work/Portfolio/Proof
   - FAQ
   - Contact
   - Booking
   - Blog/Resources
   - Legal/Policies
   - Other
2. What page is most important?
3. Do you already have copy written?
   - Yes, mostly final
   - Some rough notes
   - No, I need help shaping it
4. Do you have service descriptions ready?
5. Do you have pricing/package details ready?
6. Do you have testimonials, reviews, or client results?
7. Do you have professional photos or brand images?
8. Do any pages need special functionality?

---

## 6. Brand + Visual Direction

1. Do you have an existing logo?
2. Do you have brand colors or fonts?
3. Share links to 2–5 websites you like and what you like about them.
4. Share links to websites you dislike and what feels wrong about them.
5. Which words should describe the finished site?
   - Premium
   - Calm
   - Bold
   - Warm
   - Editorial
   - Minimal
   - Playful
   - Luxurious
   - Approachable
   - Technical
   - Other
6. Are there any colors, styles, or visual directions to avoid?
7. Should the site feel more personal, more studio/business, or a balance?

---

## 7. Proof + Trust

1. What proof can we show?
   - Testimonials
   - Reviews
   - Case studies
   - Before/after examples
   - Certifications
   - Press/features
   - Client logos
   - Photos/results
2. What are 1–3 client/customer wins you are proud of?
3. What should a skeptical visitor know about working with you?
4. Do you have any statistics, outcomes, or results we can reference honestly?

---

## 8. Logistics + Integrations

1. Do you already own a domain?
2. Where is the domain registered?
3. Where is the current website hosted, if anywhere?
4. Do you need any integrations?
   - Contact form
   - Booking/scheduling
   - Email marketing
   - Payments
   - CRM
   - Analytics
   - Chat
   - Maps/location
   - Other
5. Do you need help setting up analytics or tracking?
6. Are there legal/policy pages we need to include?
7. Are there accessibility, compliance, or industry requirements to know about?

---

## 9. Timeline + Constraints

1. Do you have a target launch date?
2. Is there an event, campaign, or deadline driving the timing?
3. Are there dates when you will be unavailable for feedback?
4. How quickly can you respond to review requests?
5. What could slow the project down?

---

## 10. Care Plan Interest

1. After launch, do you want help with updates and maintenance?
   - Yes, likely
   - Maybe, tell me more
   - Not right now
2. What ongoing support would be most useful?
   - Content updates
   - Design updates
   - Technical maintenance
   - Analytics review
   - SEO/content improvements
   - Landing pages/campaigns
3. How often do you expect the site to need updates?

---

## 11. Final Notes

1. Is there anything else Blake should know before kickoff?
2. Upload or link any helpful files:
   - Logo
   - Brand guide
   - Photos
   - Copy docs
   - Testimonials
   - Screenshots
   - Inspiration

---

## Form Settings Recommendation

- Allow save-and-return if the form platform supports it.
- Send a copy of responses to Blake.
- Send an automatic confirmation to the client.
- Use conditional fields for package-specific questions later.
- Keep the form private/unlisted and only share after deposit.

---

## Client Confirmation Copy

After submission:

> Intake received — thank you. I’ll review your answers before kickoff and use them to shape the working plan. If anything important is missing, I’ll flag it clearly so we can keep the project moving.
